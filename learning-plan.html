Learning Plan: Exploring the World of Coding

Long-term Goal or Career Pathway:

My long-term goal is to become a programmer, but I'm still exploring the different types of coding available to me. I want to gain a good understanding of the different programming languages and frameworks before deciding on a specialization.

Strengths and Limitations:

My biggest strength is my intelligence - and my familiarity with the environment that we're working in. As a weakness, I've never had to work particularly hard to learn and absorb information. As a result, I sometimes struggle with perseverance when I have to work hard to achieve what I need to. I'm committed to developing my perseverance and resilience in this programme so that I can achieve and learn in the way that I know meets my potential.

Managing Workload:

To manage my workload, I will work on developing a regular study routine that I can stick to - Ektaj really spoke to me when he mentioned that discipline is more important than motivation. I'm going to work really hard on not digging my head into the sand and shutting things out/shutting down when things get tough for me - I know that's not the right strategy, it's just a default that I need to learn to break. When things are building up and starting to get to me, I will take a break and practice self-care strategies such including exercise and talking to the people in my life who are looking out for me, and know what my challenges are.

Seeking Help:

I will seek help from the team when I need it - this is a challenge for me, but it's something I want to commit to. I will ask for clarification or additional resources if I am struggling with a concept. I'm looking forward to developing awesome working relationships with my fellow learners.

Expectations from Facilitation Team:

I expect the Facilitation Team to be able to support me with their knowledge - about both the programme and about the world of Dev.

Scheduling Information:

To my knowledge, I don't currently have any commitments that will conflict with my study time. If any commitments come up, I'll keep the Facilitation Team informed.
